CI(持续集成) 是什么

In software engineering, continuous integration (CI) is the practice of merging all developer working copies to a shared mainline several times a day.

大意: 在软件工程中，持续集成是一种这样的实践: 一天中多次将所有开发者的工作副本整合到一个共享的主线上。
